Our Profile

The Muslim Association of Meru University (MAMU) is dedicated to serving the spiritual, social, and academic needs of Muslim students and staff members within the institution.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a leading beacon of Islamic values, nurturing holistic excellence in academics and spirituality.

Mission: To provide a supportive platform for Muslim students and staff through peer mentorship, community service, and constant adherence to the Quran and Sunnah.

Constitution & Policy

Our institution works strictly under a constitution made in 2011 by the pioneers of MAMU, revised to maintain transparency and community advancement.

MAMU History

Established as the "Home of Islamic nurturing" at Meru University of Science and Technology (MUST), MAMU has structurally guided the campus Ummah for over a decade.

Patron's Message

My mission is to ensure our Muslim students have the support, resources, and guidance they need to thrive within the institution. May Allah (SWT) bless your academic journey.

Mosque Direction

The campus prayer facilities are located dynamically within the university block layouts, welcoming all students for daily congregational services.
