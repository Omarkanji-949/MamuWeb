About MAMU

The Muslim Association of Meru University (MAMU) is dedicated to serving the spiritual, social, and academic needs of Muslim students and staff members within the institution. We aim to create a supportive environment that inspires moral integrity, community service, and outstanding leadership qualities.

Official Identity & Philosophy: Operating under the tagline "Home of Islamic nurturing" (Email: mamumust@gamil.com | P.O. BOX 972-60200 Meru, Kenya), the association holds Islamic culture as its absolute bedrock. Its complete operational rules, values, and guidelines are derived directly from the Holy Quran, Hadith, and the Sunnah of the Holy Prophet Muhammad (SAW).

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a leading beacon of Islamic values, nurturing holistic excellence in academics and spirituality.

Mission: To provide a supportive platform for Muslim students and staff through peer mentorship, community service, and constant adherence to the Quran and Sunnah.

Constitution and Policy

Our institution (MAMU) works strictly under a constitution that was made on 2011 by the pioneers of MAMU.

Our History

Established as the "Home of Islamic nurturing" at Meru University of Science and Technology (MUST), MAMU has structurally guided the campus Ummah for over a decade[cite: 1].

Constitutional Timeline:

  • June 2011 (Foundation): The original MAMU Constitution was prepared and approved under Chairman Yussuf Hassan Emoy and Patron Dr. Mohamed Shano[cite: 1].
  • June 2011 (1st Revision): Instilled foundational rights, equal gender welfare protocols, and the Majlis Shura leadership vetting system[cite: 1].
  • November 2022 (2nd Revision): Updated to accommodate a growing campus population, modernized structural operations, and formalized the MAMU Alumni Department[cite: 1].

Patron's Message

"BismiAllahi arRahmani arRahim — In the Name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful"[cite: 1]

Assalamu Alaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh,

As the Patron of MAMU, it is my distinct honor to welcome all new and continuing students, faculty, and visitors to our vibrant community[cite: 1]. MAMU is affectionately known as the "Home of Islamic Nurturing," a title we hold with great responsibility[cite: 1]. Our fundamental purpose is to provide a holistic environment where academic pursuits and spiritual growth walk hand in hand[cite: 1].

In my structural role as the ex-officio link between MAMU and the university administration, my mission is to ensure our Muslim students have the support, resources, and guidance they need to thrive within the institution[cite: 1]. Whether it is coordinating campus affairs, welcoming new members, or monitoring our student executive board, we are completely committed to maintaining a safe, inclusive space for worship and personal advancement[cite: 1].

I warmly invite all Muslim students to join our active network, participate in our weekly halaqas, and build a strong foundation for their future[cite: 1]. May Allah (SWT) bless your academic journey and enrich your faith.

Dr. Mohamed Shano

Patron, Muslim Association of Meru University (MAMU)
